Work Smarter

Our bespoke digital modeling and scripting services can transform your bold concepts and design processes

At the intersection of advanced computation and traditional craft, we offer bespoke digital modeling and parametric scripting services that transform bold ideas into precise, build-ready designs. Whether you're an architect, product designer, or maker, we bridge the gap between concept and creation, delivering digital models that are as elegant as they are fabrication-ready.
Our CAD plugin, Goose, uses a heuristic-based algorithm to efficiently nest polygonal shapes within defined boundaries—a crucial step in reducing material waste and lowering production costs. From optimizing CNC milling layouts for woodworkers and metal fabricators, to streamlining production runs for small studios, this process saves time, materials, and money.
The same computational workflows that bring large-scale architectural visions to life also power our line of luxury home goods, each piece first born in code, then perfected by hand. Whether you need a fully optimized fabrication file or a one-of-a-kind object, our process blends digital precision with artisanal finish to create work that's both functional and beautiful.
