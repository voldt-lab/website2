About Us

voldt aims to create meaningful and sustainable designs that will live with you no matter where you are.


Every day, you make design decisions—some as simple as picking sneakers, others as significant as choosing an energy-efficient home—and each one helps shape your experience and the world around you.

We're two licensed architects turned computational designers who met at the Taubman School of Architecture in Ann Arbor. We recognized that data-driven design and fabrication could extend beyond buildings into the everyday objects you use.

Dedicated to lowering our carbon footprint, we use recycled/recyclable materials in additive processes that consume only what's necessary. Our innovation-focused approach delivers highly customizable forms with unique textures, all while continually seeking new ways to reduce environmental impact.
